What makes a title town?

Pudding article

Data & Abstractions

The narration of ‘ What makes a titletown’ starts with the hypothesis to determine the three key driving factors that determines th winning of a particular city . the data consisit of 458 professional sports teams from the MLB, NBA, NFL, NHL, MLS, and CFL, and 1,917 division one college teams. All told, these teams have represented 199 cities and played for 996 sports championships since 1870.


Visuals and representation

The use of graphs talk about the change in the leader board as years go by , The deflection and winning trend, change over time amongst the cities in north america.


Framing and transitions

The transitions used is helpful in making the user ubderstand the narrative between different factors affecting the victory.


Messaging and annotation

The content written helped to create the context but the annotation could have been better.


Flow and coherence

The narrative of the article was better conveyed using the sequence of the factors.


Interaction and engagement

There were not much of interaction required from the user to explore the data visulaisation, it was a scroll based engagement.


Aesthetics and design

The design of the entire pudding article had subtle and necessary colours to create impact for the data to be represented.
